Our Mission

Innovating to end hunger, achieve food security, and create a renewable sustainable food system.

THE PROBLEM

• Food waste

• Access to food

• Food preservation technology

THE SOLUTION

We could invest deeply in the core technologies that transform access to food and embed these technologies in relevant products that serve both food processing industry and families in the developing world, respectively. We could then re-invest revenues from our equipment sales into our work for the developing world, helping us incubate those emerging markets to the scale and which they could sustain their own future growth.

Our solution can scale from the farms to the fork. In other words, it can be a small unit that fits in the home to an industrial scale.

Using freeze drying technology can reduce carbon food print and create a sustainable food ecosystem.

LyoFresh

What if it was possible to reduce food waste at different scales globally by avoiding spoilage from happening in the first place? Ever since humans have harvested, we've tried to find ways to preserve it. Freeze drying is the best way to make food shelf stable without preservatives, chemicals, etc. Until now, freeze drying has never been an affordable method.
